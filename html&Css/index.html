7 TYPES OF ART

The seven different art forms are Painting, Sculpture, Literature, Architecture, Theater, Cinema and Music. However, back in the day, the seven different art forms were called the liberal Arts, consisting of Grammar, Logic Rhetoric, Arithmetic, Geometry, Astronomy, and Music.
